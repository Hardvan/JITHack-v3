Heart Attack-Major Evil Nowadays.

A heart attack is a medical emergency. A heart attack usually occurs when a blood clot blocks blood flow to the heart. Without blood, tissue loses oxygen and dies.

Can We diagonise it technically?

A handly method is implemented by us where we can predict the heart attack possibility of the person using Machine Learning and Deep Learning

Keeping Oneself Healthy

Eating an unhealthy diet that is high in fat will make hardening of the arteries (atherosclerosis) worse and increase your risk of a heart attack. Continuing to eat high-fat foods will cause more fatty plaques to build up in your arteries.

Welcome!

In a Heartbeat is a website that aims to predict whether a person is at risk of getting a heart attack or not with the power of machine learning.

Brief Intro

During recent years, the rate of heart attacks has been on the rise. In India alone, there were arround 28,449 deaths due to heart attacks. The edge that we have over other products is that we have the luxury of a pre-trained model which can predict the heart attack to a great extent.

Easy to Use

Most people are apprehensive to visit a gloom environment like that of a hospital, so our product aims to eliminate the unease and instill comfort.

Real Time

Our interactive website allows the user to enter his medical details such as oxygen %, heart rate, etc. and submit it. Our pre-trained machine learning model will accurately predict whether the person will get heart attack or not. Based on the result appropriate course of action will be provided to the users.
